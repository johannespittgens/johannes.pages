Top 5 things to see in Milan

Sempione Park

Just behind Castello Sforzesco and adjacent to the trendy Brera district, the park was designed in the late 19th century. The grounds include a gravel road, the Arch of Peace (Arco della Pace), a lake and even a small arena Shibika used for concerts and sporting events. The park's Torre Blanca Tower overlooks the entire city. The museums in the park include the Triennale Museum of Contemporary Art and Design in Palazzo dell'arte, as well as a small aquarium in the city.

Sempione Park is one of the best places to take a relaxing walk or bike in Milan and is probably part of a walking or cycling tour of the city's attractions. On sunny weekends, many city dwellers relax in the park-and there's no reason you shouldn't do the same.
